About Augen

What is Augen?

Augen is an AI-powered vision assistant designed specifically for visually impaired users. It helps you understand your surroundings by describing what's in pictures out loud.

How it works

  • Take a picture - Use your device's camera
  • AI analyzes - Advanced vision AI processes the image
  • Audio description - Hear what's in the picture through speech

What can Augen describe?

  • Text - Menus, signs, documents, labels
  • Scenes - What's happening around you
  • Objects - Items, people, animals
  • Navigation - Surroundings and obstacles

Accessibility Features

  • High contrast - Yellow and black for maximum visibility
  • Large buttons - Easy to tap with motor limitations
  • Simple interface - Just one big button to press
  • Screen reader friendly - Works with all assistive technologies
